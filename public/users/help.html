Devices

Green Dolphin supports a desktop, a mobile phone, and a tablet.

Ask a Question

You can ask your friend any question you would like to ask.

1. Write your question in a titile

2. Describe your question in a content

You can use mathematics equation by insert your equation and a format.

Σ m = (x + k)2

3. Use tags related your question to assist your friend to answer and filter questions

4. You can ask an anonymous question. You check a box that labels "Ask a question anonymously"

5. You can spend 5 points to ask lecturers and TAs for a fast question.

6. If there are expert students, you can choose their name, and Green Dolphin will send email to them automatically. You have to spend 5 points to ask expert students.

7. Click post your question and get 5 points from asking a question

Answer a Question

You can help your friends or study collaboratively

1. Select a question

2. You can answer the question anonymously by checking the checkbox

3. Write your answer and post it, you will get 10 points from answering a question. You will get 10 points when your answer is selected to be the best answer from an asker. The asker will get 5 points after you choose the best answer.

Notifications

Green Dolphin will send emails to students in a hour after students post a question. Green Dolphin sends emails to TAs after 3 hours and instructors after 6 hours.

Fast Answer

If a student selects Fast Answer, Green Dolphin will send emails to TAs and instructor immediately. Green Dolphin will send emails to students in a half hour.

Selects Experts

If a student selects an expert, Green Dolphin will send emails to the selected expert immediately. Green Dolphin will send emails to students in a half hour.

Expert Students

Expert Students are student who have much knowledge and can help you to solve a problem.

Expert students will get more 10 points, when you answer questions which are sent to you.

Reward System

You will get points when you help your friends. The leader board will show your rank in the class.

Activities Points
A student answers a question. +3
A student gets the best answer. +10
An expert answers a question which needs the expert’s help. +10
A student selects the best answer. +5
A student asks a question. +5
A student votes a useful question or answer. +3
A question or answer owner get a useful vote. +5
A student needs a fast answer. -10
A student asks a expert student. -5

Expert tags

You can add tags to yourself to show your expertise or show an identity of your group.

Your Performance

You can check your statistics at the Performance page or check your a position in the leader board at the Leaderboard page .
